Review document: https://www.w3.org/TR/2012/REC-css3-mediaqueries-20120619/

Editor's draft: https://drafts.csswg.org/mediaqueries-3/

This disposition of comments covers comments recieved since the original publication as a Recommendation on 12 June 2012 and through later revisions.

See https://www.w3.org/Style/2012/MediaQueriesDisposalOfComments.html for comments on earlier Drafts.

Issues 1 and 2 pretain to the 12 June 2012 Recommendation.

Issues 3 and 4 pretain to the updated 05 April 2022 Recommendation.

The following color coding convention is used for comments:

  • Accepted or Rejected and positive response
  • Rejected and no response
  • Rejected and negative response
  • Deferred
  • Out-of-Scope or Invalid and not verified

Open issues are marked like this

An issue can be closed as Accepted, OutOfScope, Invalid, Rejected, or Retracted. Verified indicates commentor's acceptance of the response.
